The widget is used by store partners (e.g.: affiliates) to advertise their products on their blogs, fan pages, personal pages, affiliated stores, etc.

Each page can include up to 4 widgets, each widget can display up to 6 products from the store. The partner can choose the desired design, colors and products through the parameters of the widget.


ATTENTION: Customizing this feature requires JAVASCRIPT ADVANCED KNOWLEDGE.


Follow step-by-step to create a custom widget:


1 Passo:

In the Type field, select the (*) Custom option.


2 Passo:

In the Color selection, choose the Custom option to create a widget with the desired colors.


3 Passo:

Select how many Products and Columns you want to display.


4 Passo:

To show certain products, select the Category you want and select the Only deals and Only releases fields.


5 Passo:

It is also possible to disclose the products containing the desired term.

E.G.: Enter the term iphone in the Text field

To advertise a specific product fill the field Product ID.


6 Passo:

Click the Widget preview button to see this getting your custom widget.


7 Passo:

When you have finished customizing, click the Select code button to copy the script and paste where the disclosure will be made (blogs, partner sites etc).
