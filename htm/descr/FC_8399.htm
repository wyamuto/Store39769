Here are some examples of additional fields:

  • Address reference point
  • Commercial phone
  • Mother's name
  • How did you meet the store?
  • Preferred delivery period (morning, afternoon or evening)


The fields registered in the Customer register section will be displayed in the store on the customer registration page. The fields registered in the Order delivery section will be displayed in the Dispatch date section of the order confirmation page.



The data entered in these fields will be displayed at the end of the customer master record and at the end of the data sheet for delivery in the Field 1, Field 2, and Field 3.
